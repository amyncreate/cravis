Our Story

The Home of Happiness,
Flavour & Sweet Memories

Cravins began with a simple belief: that Maiduguri deserved a dessert destination worthy of its biggest celebrations and its quietest evenings alike. From our home at Zara City Plaza on Lagos Street, we've spent years perfecting recipes, sourcing better ingredients, and building a space families return to — not because they have to, but because they want to.

Our Mission

To make every visit to Cravins feel like a small celebration — through premium ingredients, genuine hospitality, and flavours you won't find anywhere else in the city.

Our Promise

Customer-first, always. Every scoop is made with care, every order is treated like it matters — because to us, it does.

  • Premium, carefully-sourced ingredients in every recipe
  • A warm, family-friendly space built for real gatherings
  • Consistent quality, visit after visit
  • Genuine hospitality from a team that knows your name
